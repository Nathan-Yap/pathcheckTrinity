  • [App Name]

    We aim to build a crowdsourced application for epidemiology without compromising on the privacy of citizens to mitigate any pandemic within 100 days. All the crowdsourced information will be further augmented by data from sensors in mobile phones, weather data, mobility data, social media feeds (publicly available), and more. This will enable us to share insights (aggregated, privacy preserved) with public health officials and policymakers to empower them to make informed decisions about the non-pharmaceutical interventions (NPIs).

    Crowdsourcing

    Through this application, users are incentivized to share their data onto the app, being incentivised by getting health information, understanding health anomalies in the locality (analogous to traffic blocks on the route), gaining actionable insights on what to do to make sure they are safe (based on risk calculators powered by powerful ABM/ML models that simulate various scenarios and under various circumstances - office, theatre, beach, concert and more) to incentives such as privileged access to venues, monitory benefits, and more.

    Privacy

    Users can share prior medical history, vaccination logs, etc., so we have the immune profile of the population and also the high-level knowledge of the spatiotemporal patterns of various diseases and conditions similar to the pandemic (or epidemic) or lifestyle diseases (diabetics, pressure, etc). All this information can be shared confidently as we provide multiple layers of protection/privacy to the user.
